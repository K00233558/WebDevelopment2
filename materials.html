Here at S.J. Storage Solutions we use only the best of material, you'll find a range of different materials and colours to choose from below.

We use the very latest smooth running sliding track technology available, combined with stylish materials from traditional timber coloured panels to the ultra modern lacobel coloured glass (all of which are safety backed and manufactured to the BS 6206 quality standard).

Our profiles are made from the heaviest Grade alluminium in the Market designed to take 18mm panels compared to the standard 10mm depth so therefore with almost twice the thickness as standard systems.

Be as creative as you like! Choose the style of panelling you would like on your door from 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5 below and then combine any number of the materials below to create your dream wardrobe!
