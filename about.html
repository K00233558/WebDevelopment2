S.J. Storage Solutions is run by Stephen Joyce and based in Limerick. Stephen's father has been in the carpentry business for many years and was the owner of Joyce's Kitchens a well known name in Limerick and the surrounding areas. Stephen has worked with his Dad, Brendan in Joyce's Kitchens for the past sixteen years and is now also starting his own business, S.J. Storage Solutions. Stephen takes great pride in his work and is passionate about carpentry, this can clearly be seen in the pieces he produces. S.J. Storage Solutions is a one stop shop for sliding wardrobes, custom wardrobes, under stairs storage solutions and custom furniture. All the pieces are made in the family workshop and delivered and fit by Stephen personally.

Have a look through the website and if you have any questions on any of the products you see just give Stephen a call, 087 3936264.
