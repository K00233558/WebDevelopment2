How do I go about getting a quote?

Ring Steve on 087 3936264 with your address and we will call to you at your home to measure and advise you on the options available (approximately one week waiting time)

Do you offer a free advice / consultation service?

Yes! All quotations are free and there is absolutely no obligation to purchase. We will call to your home and advise you on the different options, free of charge.

Are all your wardrobes standard depth and height?

No, all our wardrobes are custom made and can be made to any depth and height. The ideal depth we like to use is 650mm and the ideal height is floor to ceiling.

How long does it take to install by fitters?

A small wardrobe usually a couple of hours, a large wardrobe a full day

What is the procedure regarding payment?

We accept cash, cheques, visa/mastercard and laser(debit cards). We ask for 50% on order and the remaining 50% on completion. You can pay the balance to the installation crew on the installation day.

What size doors do you provide?

Any width from 500mm to 1220mm and any height from 900mm to 2540mm.

Can you fit into sloping ceilings?

Yes we can make our doors to fit attic/dormer ceilings in most cases.

Do you fit corner wardrobes?

Yes no problem, we have a useful space saving hanging system specifically for corner situations.

Do you fit under stairs storage?

Yes we have been installing storage solutions under stairs for many years now. We have a variety of solutions on offer.
